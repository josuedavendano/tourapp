Costs

There are no joining fees. The pricings for your tours are up to you. You automatically get paid once the transaction is successful. We only charge a 3% service fee.


Requirement

The only requirement to join us is to have an NYC tour guide license. This way tourists know that they are being shown NYC by serious and experienced tour guides.


Flexibility

We know New Yorkers are constantly hustling, so you get to set up your schedule! You also determine the maximum amount of people you would like to take on your tour.
